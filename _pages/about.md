Hi, there! I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Artificial Intelligence at [LMU Munich](https://www.lmu.de/en/), advised by Prof. Volker Tresp. My research focuses on **agent memory, agentic RL, multi-agent systems, agent harnessing, and multimodal agents**.

I am also a researcher at the [Munich Center for Machine Learning (MCML)](https://mcml.ai/) — one of Germany's six national AI Competence Centers.

Previously, I earned my M.Sc. in Informatics at LMU Munich and my B.Sc. in Informatics at TUM Munich. 

I love mentoring and regularly supervise motivated Master's and Bachelor's students, and I always do my best to help them grow and land the strongest possible next position. If you'd like to work with me on agents, memory, or agentic RL, feel free to reach out by [email](mailto:yan@dbs.ifi.lmu.de) — I'm always happy to chat. You can also see where my [past students](#students) are now.
